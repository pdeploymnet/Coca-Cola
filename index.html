Coca-Cola, Open Happiness!

Coca-Cola is a globally renowned beverage company founded in 1886 in Atlanta, Georgia. With its iconic Coca-Cola soda as its flagship product, the company offers a diverse portfolio of non-alcoholic beverages, including Diet Coke, Sprite, Fanta, and various fruit juices. Known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability, Coca-Cola operates in over 200 countries, delighting consumers worldwide with refreshing and iconic beverages. The company's strong brand presence, extensive distribution network, and dedication to corporate responsibility have made it a leading player in the beverage industry.
