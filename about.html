Name: The Coca-Cola Company

Founding Year: 1886

Headquarters: Atlanta, Georgia, USA

Industry: Beverage

Products: The Coca-Cola Company offers a diverse range of non-alcoholic beverages. Its flagship product, Coca-Cola, is an internationally recognized and beloved carbonated soft drink. The company's product portfolio includes Diet Coke, Sprite, Fanta, Minute Maid fruit juices, Powerade sports drinks, Dasani water, and a variety of other beverages to suit consumers' preferences across the globe.

Global Presence: With a rich history spanning over a century, Coca-Cola has grown into a global beverage leader. Operating in more than 200 countries, the company has established a vast and intricate distribution network, making its products widely accessible to billions of consumers worldwide.

Mission Statement: Coca-Cola's mission is "To refresh the world, inspire moments of optimism and happiness, and create value and make a difference." This mission reflects the company's commitment to bringing joy and refreshment to people's lives while contributing positively to society and stakeholders.

Core Values: Coca-Cola's core values guide its actions and decisions. These values include integrity, upholding the highest ethical standards; respect, valuing diversity and treating everyone with dignity; accountability, taking responsibility for actions and impacts; quality, delivering the highest standard of products; and innovation, continuously seeking new and creative ways to meet consumer needs.

Sustainability Commitment: As a socially responsible corporate citizen, Coca-Cola places significant emphasis on sustainability. The company actively works towards water stewardship, aiming to replenish the water used in its beverages and production processes. Additionally, Coca-Cola is committed to sustainable packaging, recycling initiatives, climate action, and community development, contributing positively to the environment and the communities it serves.

Brand Recognition: Coca-Cola's red and white logo is one of the most recognized and valuable brands globally. The brand's timeless appeal, memorable advertising campaigns, and consistent quality have contributed to its iconic status, making it synonymous with moments of happiness, celebration, and togetherness worldwide.
